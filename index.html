Gattaway Farmhouse is a Georgian period country house offering a comfortable rural retreat in the heart of Scotland. The historic architecture is complemented by full modern amenities and all our letting rooms have private en-suites.

Our hillside location in the picturesque conservation village of Abernethy offers stunning panoramic views of the Tay and Earn valleys and beyond. We are ideally placed for exploring the many and varied attractions of Scotland, from golf to fishing and hunting, hill walking, mountain biking, historical buildings and castles, beautiful scenery and more.

A warm welcome awaits you in this beautiful corner of Perthshire!
