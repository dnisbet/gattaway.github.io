Privacy Statement:
We need to retain records of personal data for a variety of reasons. For example if you book a room with us then we need to have a note of your name and/or other contact details so that we know who you are when you arrive or are able to contact you with information about your stay. We also retain information such as names and contact details after your stay if we need to contact you for some reason, for example if we discover you have left an item of luggage behind. We are also required to keep records of business activity for tax reporting reasons which could include personal information relating to bookings and some of this information may therefore, in the course of normal business practice, be passed on to trusted third parties such as an accountant. We are also required to keep a register of guests over the age of 16 who stay with us, this includes full names and nationality, and/or passport numbers, place of issue, details of next destination if they are non-British, Irish or Commonwealth guests. This is in accordance with the (Immigration (Hotel records) Order 1972). These records are kept for a minimum of 12 months and in accordance with the DPA (Data Protection Act 1998) and the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

Our policy with regards to any personal data we receive as part of any booking or enquiry through this website or third party website or any other channel, is that we will endeavour to keep this information secure and confidential and will only contact you for the purposes of anything we reasonably deem to be pertinent to your past, present or future stays with us. We will not pass on your information to anyone for marketing purposes. In the case of third party booking providers etc, they will have their own policies which will also apply.
Information regarding your rights with respect to Data Protection is available here:  https://ico.org.uk/for-the-public/ and if you wish to exercise any such rights please contact us using the above information.

Cookies:
We do not receive, store, or use any information from cookies on this website.
